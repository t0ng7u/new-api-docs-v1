<Callout type="warn" title="Important Note">
  In New API, Xinference's rerank response structure will be formatted as Jina's rerank response structure, and its usage will be the same as Jina's rerank. **For client users like Dify**: When configuring, please select **Jina AI** as the provider type instead of Xinference, and use Xinference-supported model names.
</Callout>

## üìù Introduction

Xinference's rerank API is fully compatible with Jina AI's rerank API. Please refer to the [Jina AI Rerank Format](jinaai-rerank.md) documentation for detailed usage, request parameters, and response format.

## üí° Usage

When using the Xinference rerank API, simply set the `model` parameter to a Xinference-supported rerank model; the remaining parameters and usage are the same as the Jina AI rerank API.

### Example Request

```bash
curl https://your-newapi-server-address/v1/rerank \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer $NEWAPI_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "model": "jina-reranker-v2",
    "query": "What is the capital of the United States?",
    "documents": [
      "The capital of Nevada is Carson City.",
      "The Northern Mariana Islands are a group of islands in the Pacific Ocean, and their capital is Saipan.",
      "Washington, D.C. (also known as Washington or D.C., officially the District of Columbia) is the capital of the United States.",
      "Capitalization in English grammar is the use of capital letters at the beginning of words. English usage differs from capitalization in other languages.",
      "Capital punishment has existed in the United States since before it became a country. As of 2017, capital punishment is legal in 30 of the 50 states."
    ],
    "top_n": 3
  }'
```

For more details, please refer to the [Jina AI Rerank Format](jinaai-rerank.md) documentation.
